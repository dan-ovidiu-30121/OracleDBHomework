Practice 6.3
1. FULL OUTER JOIN
2. outer join
3. LEFT OUTER JOIN
4. RIGHT OUTER JOIN
5. inner join

Try it/Solve it
1. SELECT e.FIRST_NAME,e.LAST_NAME,d.DEPARTMENT_NAME 
   FROM EMPLOYEES e LEFT OUTER JOIN DEPARTMENTS d
   ON (e.DEPARTMENT_ID = d.DEPARTMENT_ID);
2. SELECT e.FIRST_NAME,e.LAST_NAME,d.DEPARTMENT_NAME 
   FROM EMPLOYEES e RIGHT OUTER JOIN DEPARTMENTS d
   ON (e.DEPARTMENT_ID = d.DEPARTMENT_ID);
3. SELECT e.FIRST_NAME,e.LAST_NAME,d.DEPARTMENT_NAME 
   FROM EMPLOYEES e FULL OUTER JOIN DEPARTMENTS d
   ON (e.DEPARTMENT_ID = d.DEPARTMENT_ID);
4. SELECT c.FIRST_NAME, c.LAST_NAME, e.EVENT_DATE, e.DESCRIPTION
   FROM D_CLIENTS c LEFT OUTER JOIN D_EVENTS e
   ON (c.CLIENT_NUMBER = e.CLIENT_NUMBER);
5. SELECT a.SHIFT_ASSIGN_DATE, s.DESCRIPTION
   FROM F_SHIFT_ASSIGNMENTS a RIGHT OUTER JOIN F_SHIFTS s
   ON (a.CODE = s.CODE);
