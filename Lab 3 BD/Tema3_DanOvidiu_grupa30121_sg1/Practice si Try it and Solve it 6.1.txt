Practice 6.1
1. cross join
2. natural join

Try it/Solve it
1. SELECT LAST_NAME, DEPARTMENT_NAME
   FROM EMPLOYEES CROSS JOIN DEPARTMENTS;
2. SELECT DEPARTMENT_ID, DEPARTMENT_NAME, LOCATION_ID, CITY
   FROM DEPARTMENTS NATURAL JOIN LOCATIONS;
3. SELECT DEPARTMENT_ID, DEPARTMENT_NAME, LOCATION_ID, CITY
   FROM DEPARTMENTS NATURAL JOIN LOCATIONS
   WHERE DEPARTMENT_ID BETWEEN 20 AND 50;
